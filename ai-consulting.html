FAQ

Before the diagnosis.

How is this different from an AI training?+

A training teaches tools. HOTSTUFF designs an adoption system: diagnosis, roadmap, Build Up Day, prototypes, and follow-up.

What if my team knows nothing about AI?+

No problem. The diagnostic identifies who knows what and how to build balanced teams.

How much time does it take?+

The Build Up Day takes a full day. Before that there are 2–3 short diagnosis and design meetings.

How much does it cost?+

It's quoted based on team size, diagnosis, Build Up Day length, and level of follow-up.
